Overview

Online Bank Fund Transfer

About

As a part of my Google UX Certification Program , I chose my Second project as Web Banking Fund Transfer . Being a commerce graduate I wanted to develop expertise in the finance domain with regard to UX Principles.

Fin -Tech plays a major role in economic value. The app largely focuses on multiway balance transfer gateway. Web App helps users for a fast & quick money transfer.

Hypothesis

Uprising of UPI and other Online Payment Platforms has created revolution in the Financial Market , Which has made the necessity for the simplification and accessible and easy methods of online banking transfer

Information Architecture

UX-Implementation

  • Minimalistic Design
  • Get Salt Priciples
  • Hicks Law

  • Goal - Gradient Effect
  • Visibility of System Status
  • Fitt's Law

Research

Conducted User interviews and created user map to gain user insights. ‍

84% of users feel that the navigation and user-flow is very hard.
55% of users feel , the banking terminologies & jargons are hard to understand.
94% of users think that the banking products emphasis too much on security than user needs.
