Movie Ticket Booking App

About

As a part of my Google UX Certification Program , I chose my first project as Ticket Booking App for a Movie Theater . I took this project over other with my interest to solve edge cases and enhance the app with much more accessibility .

Movies are part of our culture in INDIA .With almost 1.4 Billion in Population, the uncertainties are at peak which creates room for Edge Cases. A good UX design can always solve edge cases enhance the product and make it accessible to all .

Hypothesis

People and Technology go hand in hand , Technology has become a part of our lives to an extent were we expect tech to solve human pain points in some way . Ticket Booking app has come a long way , this app is all about understanding people's pain points , enhancing user experience and resolving edge cases.

Research

Conducted User Survey to gain user insights. I wrote short survey question and posted it online with different communities.

80% of users wished to have in-built wallet payment.
90% of users wanted postponement of ticket to alternate show instead of cancellation.
94% of users are inclined understand user pains.
