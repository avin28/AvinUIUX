Overview

Food App For Visually Challenged

About

As a part of my Google UX Certification Program , I chose my third project as Food App For Blind. I took this project with the interest of exploring accessibility features and build technology all-inclusive.

Technology plays a role in almost all sections of human life. Technology must be inclusive to everyone.

Hypothesis

Accessibility in Technology can do wonders . A Tech designed with Equity and Equality in mind will always be avail to all.

Solutions

Solutions derived after research

  • Dynamic & Static CTA buttons (People who are visually challenged have the habit of memorizing nav flow and points)
  • Re-order pop-up option
  • Voice-over Nav flow
  • Voice-update on order ( For Tracking)
  • Voice Instruct option ( To speak with delivery assistant)
